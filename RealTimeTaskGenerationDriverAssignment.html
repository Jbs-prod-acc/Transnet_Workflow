Driver Assignment Workflow Steps Automated Assignment Logic

The system follows this flow to allocate and manage drivers effectively:

Trip Trigger

Trip creation or schedule update initiates a search for qualified, rested drivers.

Driver Matching

System filters drivers based on qualifications, hours of rest, and region familiarity.

Assignment Dispatch

Assigned drivers receive push alerts and must confirm readiness.

Shift Logging

Each assignment is logged against HR compliance and shift dashboards.

Feedback & Notes

Driver notes and feedback are captured post-shift to refine future assignments.

BI Optimization

Driver performance trends are visualized to optimize future allocations.
