SMS Alert Workflow Notification Chain Logic

Automated SMS alerts are triggered based on real-time events across operations and linked actors within the Mobility App and Enterprise System.

Trigger Event Target Recipient Message Content Status
Driver enters unsafe zone Safety Officer, Corridor Manager “⚠️ Alert: Driver Fabchi entered Zone 4A – Delay over 45 mins.” Critical
Maintenance call-out submitted Mobile Admin, Maintenance Coordinator “🛠️ Alert: Call-out logged near Koedoespoort Yard at 13:45.” High
Driver logs first check-in Shift Supervisor “✅ Check-in recorded: Officer Paris, Zone Alpha at 06:12.” Normal
Unauthorized stop detected Control Room, Dispatch Team “🚨 Unexpected stop on Iron Ore Route – Confirm crew status.” Critical
All messages are logged in the BI SharePoint database and accessible via the Notification Logs dashboard. Message templates are customizable.
