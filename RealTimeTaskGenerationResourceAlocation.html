Resource Allocation Workflow Steps Assignment Protocol

Outlined below is the step-by-step resource allocation logic:

Demand Detection

System flags under-resourced corridors or operational overload based on live task inflow.

Resource Matching

Available staff/equipment are filtered based on skill, location, and shift availability.

Smart Reallocation

System suggests optimal reassignments or alerts administrator if manual action is required.

Reassignment Execution

Affected teams are notified and GPS tracking is updated to reflect new deployments.

Workflow Logging

Every reallocation event is time-stamped and recorded for audit and training reference.

Performance Feedback

Post-task evaluations are used to enhance future staff deployment decisions.

This allocation engine boosts operational efficiency, prevents corridor overload, and promotes smart manpower planning.
