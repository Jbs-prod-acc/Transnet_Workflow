Incident Trigger Workflow Steps Automated Monitoring

This section outlines how incident triggers are identified, evaluated, escalated, and optimized throughout terminal operations.

Data Monitoring

Live yard congestion, fuel usage, and dwell time metrics are tracked from IoT sensors and terminal logs.

Threshold Evaluation

Metrics are evaluated against defined limits (e.g., 85% congestion, 240 mins dwell time).

Trigger Detected

Alerts are automatically triggered and sent to responsible teams via the notification chain.

Escalation & Assignment

Personnel are assigned tasks automatically in the workflow with priority escalation flags.

Workflow Logging

Every action is logged for audit trails, reviews, and compliance documentation.

BI Feedback Loop

Insights from resolved incidents are fed into dashboards for continuous process optimization.

This process ensures reduced human oversight, faster responses, and enhanced accountability in incident handling.
