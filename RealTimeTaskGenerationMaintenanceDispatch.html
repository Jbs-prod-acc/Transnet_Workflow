Maintenance Dispatch Workflow Steps Operational Task Chain

This section outlines how maintenance issues are detected, escalated, assigned, and resolved within the Transnet freight environment.

Issue Detection

Maintenance alerts are generated from driver submissions or system diagnostics (e.g., overheating, brake faults).

Alert Notification

The system sends alerts to regional maintenance hubs and the dispatch controller for review.

Technician Dispatch

Mobile maintenance units are assigned based on proximity, skillset, and workload capacity.

Repair & Feedback

Technicians perform on-site repairs and submit task closure notes via mobile platform.

Workflow Logging

All dispatch actions, resolutions, and timestamps are logged for audit and compliance tracking.

BI Optimization

Maintenance patterns are visualized to improve scheduling, resource planning, and preventive maintenance.

The dispatch workflow ensures rapid issue resolution, asset uptime, and operational safety throughout the rail network.
